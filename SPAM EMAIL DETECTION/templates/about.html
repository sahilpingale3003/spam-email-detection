📧 Email Classifications

Our AI-powered system detects and classifies emails into three categories based on their content and patterns.

✅

Legitimate

Safe and trustworthy emails from verified sources. These emails show no signs of malicious intent or suspicious patterns.

  • Professional sender address
  • Clear and relevant content
  • No suspicious links or attachments
⚠️

Spam

Unsolicited promotional emails or unwanted bulk messages. While not always harmful, they can clutter your inbox.

  • Promotional or marketing content
  • Urgency tactics used
  • Unknown or suspicious sender
🚨

Phishing

Dangerous emails attempting to steal personal information or credentials. These require immediate attention and caution.

  • Impersonates trusted entities
  • Contains suspicious links
  • Requests sensitive information
